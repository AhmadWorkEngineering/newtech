Newtech Pipes Quality Policy

Possesses a Quality Control System that operates with a fully automatic control mechanism, and that begins from the stage of procuring raw materials to the final stage of pipe manufacture. It is presented to the consumer with complete assurance with impermeability, external load resistance, and environmental protection factors reinforced a lot more at each stage. Without exception, each product that has completed the manufacture process meticulously undergoes all the tests stated in the international standards .
