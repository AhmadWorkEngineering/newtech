About NewTech

Newtech is established since 2000 to meet the ever increasing demand of quality pipes & allied fittings for the water delivery and distribution industry as well as for the Gas distribution systems. It is today a leading Pipe manufacturer in the country. As a major player in the industry, our Company has evolved to be synonymous with quality through its comprehensive range of high-end products marketed under the brand name Newtech.

The company manufactures the High Density Poly Ethylene (HDPE), Unplasticized Poly Vinyl Chloride (UPVC) & Polypropylene Random Copolymer (PPRC) Pipes under the popular brand name Newtech. It is Authorised Agent for Internationally well-established fitting manufacturers and import & provide their products for our customers’ convenience.

Newtech accomplishes the quality of its products through an adopted and certified integrated quality management system in accordance with ISO 9001-2008 Standards and production control system in accordance with the ISO Standards as well as through periodic tests in accredited laboratories. Its has certification from Pakistan Standards & Quality Control Authority and Pakistan Engineering Council as both of these organizations are engaged in the development of plastic industry in Pakistan.

The raw materials for production are imported from first-class World suppliers, which guarantee high quality of our products.

Most of our valued clients including government & private sector organizations, iNGOs, Consultants, Developers and Contractors who satisfied with our products & services. We are in agreement with various UN subsidiaries & International NGOs for procurement of HDPE Pipes & fittings for their various projects regarding emergency relief program in Pakistan. We have the facility to complete the given assignments on turn-key basis as well.

Our products are widely used in Water Distribution, gas conveyance, protection of optical cables, Construction, Government Enterprises, Irrigation, Agriculture, Industry and other purposes.

Newtech is a fast growing, dynamic company led by true visionaries. The company is driven in its vision “Committed to Excellence”, by its core values of integrity, responsibility and commitment to the customers and society. The company is a family of like-minded & hardworking people. We firmly believe in an open work culture that encourages teamwork and healthy communication, at the same time nurturing individuals’ growth and talent.
